Meaning Systems Pattern

Religion, philosophy, ideology, media, and AI are subtypes of the same Information / Meaning layer. They determine how civilizations explain reality, legitimize authority, coordinate action, and fracture under stress.

  • Unification: shared myths, scriptures, laws, and ideologies stabilize large groups.
  • Expansion: new media technologies spread ideas beyond local memory.
  • Fragmentation: competing interpretations split shared reality.
  • Collapse risk: meaning failure amplifies political, biological, climate, and technological shocks.
