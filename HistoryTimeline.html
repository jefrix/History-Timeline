Collapse Signature Pattern

The Collapse Case Studies layer tracks the research project's primary historical analogues — civilizations that reached peak complexity, then collapsed through legitimacy failure, infrastructure fragility, or meaning-system breakdown.

  • Beiyang Fleet (1888–1895): prestige artifact concealing structural fragility — the synthesis's primary Anthropic node analogue.
  • Self-Strengthening vs. Meiji: surface modernization vs. institutional transformation — the quasi-experimental control pair.
  • Young Turk / Ottoman: failed modernization rising — political transformation without structural renewal.
  • Meaning Systems Pattern: Religion, philosophy, ideology, media, and AI are treated as subtypes of the Information / Meaning layer.
