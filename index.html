Welcome to my history timeline, which begins with the Neolithic era and traces the development of human societies, languages, and religions through the Bronze Age and beyond. The shift to agriculture in the Neolithic era gave rise to early civilizations and had a profound impact on the evolution of languages and religions. As people settled, their languages and belief systems diversified and adapted to their new environments. The Bronze Age brought technological advancements, trade, and migration, further shaping the development of languages, religions, and cultures around the world. My timeline focuses on the Bronze Age Collapse and the dawn of the Iron Age, and I aim to provide a brief view of the intricate connections and patterns that have shaped human history, shedding light on the ever-evolving tapestry of human experiences and beliefs.
